Additional Information
& FAQ

How can I request a cleaning service?
Scheduling a cleaning service with us is easy! Simply fill out the online booking form. You can also give us a call or send us an email, and our friendly team will assist you in scheduling a convenient appointment.
How do you determine the cost of cleaning services?
The cost of our cleaning services depends on factors such as the size of the space, the type of cleaning required, and any additional services requested. We offer transparent pricing, and you can check the services section on our website or by contacting our customer service.
How long does a typical cleaning service take?
The duration of our cleaning services varies based on factors such as the size of the space, the level of cleaning required, and any specific requests you may have. Our team will provide you with an estimated timeframe during the booking process, and we strive to complete our services efficiently without compromising on quality. If you have time constraints or specific scheduling needs, please let us know, and we will do our best to accommodate them.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning service?
It's completely up to you. Many of our clients prefer to be present during the first cleaning, but our professional and trustworthy team can perform their duties efficiently whether you're at home or not. We'll discuss your preferences during the booking process.
How do you ensure the safety of my personal belongings?
Our team is trained to handle your belongings with care and respect. We take all necessary precautions to ensure the safety and security of your personal items during the cleaning process.
What areas do you serve?
Tabys INC proudly serves all the 5 boroughs of New York city. Contact us to confirm if we cover your specific location.
Can I customize my cleaning service to focus on specific areas or tasks?
Absolutely! We understand that each client's needs are unique. You can customize your cleaning service to address specific areas or tasks that matter most to you. Just let us know your preferences during the booking process.
